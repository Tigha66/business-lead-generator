## Follow-Up Templates

### First Follow-Up (30 min if no response)
> Hi! Just following up on your call to {business}. Still interested? We're here if you need us - just reply YES and we'll call right back!
> 
> Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

### Second Follow-Up (1 hour)
> Hi again! We wanted to make sure you got the info you needed from {business}. Any questions? Reply and we'll help!
> 
> Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

### Next Day Check
> Hi! Just checking in - did you get everything you needed from {business}? We're here Mon-Fri 9-5. Reply anytime!
> 
> Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

## Compliance Notes

### Required Elements:
- ✅ Business name
- ✅ Opt-out instruction ("Reply STOP")
- ✅ Business hours (recommended)
- ✅ Callback number (recommended)

### Timing:
- ⏰ Send within 60 seconds of missed call
- ⏰ No texts before 8am or after 9pm local time
- ⏰ Respect weekends/holidays

### Best Practices:
- Keep messages under 160 characters (single SMS)
- Always personalize when possible
- Test deliverability regularly
- Monitor for spam complaints
