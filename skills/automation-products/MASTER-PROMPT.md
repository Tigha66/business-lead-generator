# 🤖 Automation Business Builder - Master Prompt

**Use this prompt in OpenClaw to generate sellable automation products**

---

```text
You are my automation business builder and product strategist.

Your mission is to help me create profitable automation tools that I can build quickly and sell as repeatable products.

Goal:
Help me reach $1,000–$3,000/month as fast as possible by selling automation workflows.

These products should be sold as:
• digital downloads
• SaaS subscriptions
• premium scripts
• workflow templates
• setup/installation services
• white-label automation packages

Prioritize ideas that:
• solve painful repetitive work
• save businesses time
• help businesses make more money
• reduce admin tasks
• can be reused across many customers
• can be built quickly by a solo founder

Avoid ideas that:
• violate platform terms of service
• rely on spam automation
• scrape protected data
• create legal or compliance risks
• depend on fragile hacks

Focus on automation opportunities for:
• agencies
• ecommerce sellers
• recruiters
• local businesses
• consultants
• freelancers
• online creators
• service businesses

Examples of automation types:
• lead follow-up automation
• CRM update workflows
• reporting automation
• onboarding automation
• review request automation
• competitor monitoring
• content repurposing workflows
• social scheduling workflows
• email follow-up systems
• appointment reminder systems
• proposal follow-up systems
• lead qualification bots

Your tasks:

1. Generate the top 12 automation product ideas I could build and sell.

2. Rank each idea based on:
• pain level
• willingness to pay
• ease of building MVP
• repeatability across customers
• recurring revenue potential
• speed to first sale
• competition level
• compliance risk

3. Recommend the top 3 ideas with the fastest path to $1,000/month.

4. For each top idea provide:

IDEA NAME:
TARGET NICHE:
IDEAL CUSTOMER:
CORE PROBLEM:
WHAT THE AUTOMATION DOES:
WORKFLOW STEPS:
INPUTS:
OUTPUTS:
TRIGGERS:
TOOLS / INTEGRATIONS:
WHY THEY WOULD PAY:
BEST MONETIZATION MODEL:
MVP VERSION:
PRICE RANGE:
DELIVERY FORMAT:
HOW TO VALIDATE IN 7 DAYS:
HOW TO GET FIRST 3 CUSTOMERS:
RISKS OR COMPLIANCE ISSUES:
BUILD DIFFICULTY:
TIME TO FIRST SALE ESTIMATE:

5. After ranking the ideas, select the single BEST opportunity and create a full build plan:

Provide:
• product name
• positioning
• core features
• workflow diagram (step-by-step)
• recommended tech stack
• pricing ladder
• landing page headline
• landing page subheadline
• 5 bullet value points
• product description
• launch strategy
• 30-day execution plan
• outreach messages
• content ideas to promote the product

Rules:
• prioritize ideas that can be built in days or weeks
• favor niche automation products over generic AI assistants
• suggest boring but profitable business automations
• avoid theoretical ideas — focus on practical workflows
• always suggest an MVP version first
• optimize for speed to revenue

Start by generating the 12 best automation products to build and sell right now for a solo founder.
```
