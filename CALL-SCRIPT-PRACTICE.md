# 📞 Call Script Practice - Objection Handling

**Target:** UK Small Businesses (Pubs, Salons, Restaurants, Trades)
**Goal:** Book website sales (£397 one-time)

---

## 🎯 The Opening (First 10 Seconds)

```
"Hi, is this [Owner/Manager name if you have it]?

Hi, this is Tigha. I'm a local web developer and I noticed 
[Business Name] doesn't have a website yet.

I help businesses like yours get found on Google and attract more customers.

Do you have a quick minute to chat about this?"
```

**Pause and wait for response.**

---

## 🏆 Common Objections & Responses

### 1️⃣ "It's too expensive" / "We can't afford it"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "It's actually really cheap" or "You can afford it"

**✅ SAY:**
```
"I totally understand budget is important. Let me ask you this:

How much is a typical [job/customer/order] worth to you?

[Let them answer - e.g., "£50-100 for a meal" or "£200 for a plumbing job"]

Right. So if the website brings you just 4-5 new customers, it's already paid for itself.

And the thing is - most people search Google before they call. 
If you're not there, they're calling your competitor instead.

So really, can you afford NOT to have a website?"
```

**Follow-up:**
```
"I can also do a payment plan if that helps - £200 now, £197 when the site goes live.
Would that work better?"
```

---

### 2️⃣ "I don't need a website" / "We're doing fine without one"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "You're wrong" or "Everyone needs a website"

**✅ SAY:**
```
"That's great that you're doing well! Sounds like you've got a solid business.

Can I ask - where do most of your customers find you right now?

[Let them answer]

Interesting. So here's the thing - I was looking for [their type of business] 
in [their city] earlier, and I noticed you didn't show up. Your competitors did though.

Most people search Google first before they call. If you're not there, 
you're missing out on customers who are actively looking for what you offer.

A website isn't about replacing what's working - it's about getting EVEN MORE customers
without any extra work from you.

Would you be open to seeing what that could look like?"
```

---

### 3️⃣ "I'm too busy" / "Now's not a good time"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "This will only take a minute" (it won't)

**✅ SAY:**
```
"Perfect! That actually tells me business is good - you're busy serving customers.

Here's why I'm calling: a website actually helps you get MORE customers 
without you having to do any extra work.

I handle everything - design, content, photos, the whole thing.
You don't need to lift a finger.

And once it's live, it works for you 24/7 - even when you're closed.

I can have it done in about a week. Would next week work for a quick follow-up call?"
```

**Alternative close:**
```
"Tell you what - I'll send you an email with some examples of similar businesses 
I've worked with. Take a look when you have time, and I'll follow up in a few days.

What's the best email to reach you at?"
```

---

### 4️⃣ "Let me think about it" / "I need to discuss with my partner"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "Okay, call me when you decide" (you'll never hear back)

**✅ SAY:**
```
"Totally understand - it's a decision worth thinking about.

Can I ask - is there anything specific you're unsure about? 
Is it the price, the timeline, or something else?

[Listen to their concern and address it]

Here's what I'll do - I'll send you an email with:
- Examples of similar websites I've built
- Exactly what's included
- My contact info if you have questions

Take your time to look it over. I'll follow up in a few days to see what you think.

What's the best email to reach you at?"
```

**Then GET THEIR EMAIL and actually send it.**

---

### 5️⃣ "I already have someone" / "We have a web guy"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "They're probably overcharging you"

**✅ SAY:**
```
"That's great! It's smart to have someone looking after your online presence.

Can I ask - when did you last update the site? 

[Let them answer]

Got it. Here's the thing - I offer a free second opinion. 
No charge, no obligation.

I'll take a look at your current site and let you know if there's anything 
that could be improved to get you more customers.

Worst case, you get some free advice. Best case, I might be able to help you 
get even better results.

Would you be open to that?"
```

---

### 6️⃣ "Send me some info" / "Email me details"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "Sure, I'll send something" (and never follow up)

**✅ SAY:**
```
"Absolutely - I'll send you some examples and pricing.

Before I do though, can I ask - what's most important to you in a website? 
Is it getting more customers, looking more professional, or something else?

[Listen - this tells you what to emphasize in your follow-up]

Perfect. I'll include that in the email.

I'll send it over today, and I'll follow up on [specific day - e.g., Thursday] 
to see what you think.

Does [their email] work best, or is there a better email to use?"
```

**KEY:** Set a specific follow-up date and ACTUALLY follow up.

---

### 7️⃣ "We tried a website before and it didn't work"

**❌ DON'T SAY:** "Well, this time will be different"

**✅ SAY:**
```
"I hear that a lot, and I'm sorry to hear it didn't work out.

Can I ask - what kind of website was it? Was it more like a brochure, 
or did it actually try to get you customers?

[Let them answer]

Yeah, that's the problem with a lot of websites - they look nice but 
they don't actually DO anything.

The websites I build are designed to do one thing: get you more customers.

- Mobile-friendly (most people search on phones)
- Shows up on Google
- Makes it easy for customers to contact you
- Loads fast so people don't leave

It's not about having a website - it's about having a website that WORKS.

Would you be open to seeing the difference?"
```

---

### 8️⃣ "What if it doesn't work?" / "What's the guarantee?"

**✅ SAY:**
```
"That's a fair question.

Here's what I can guarantee:
- The site will be live and working within a week
- It will be mobile-friendly and show up on Google
- You'll get a contact form so customers can reach you

What I can't guarantee is exactly how many customers you'll get - 
that depends on your business, your location, and your competition.

But here's the thing - if your website brings you just ONE new customer 
per month, it's already paid for itself.

And I include 30 days of free support after launch - if anything needs 
tweaking, I'll fix it at no charge.

Does that sound fair?"
```

## 🎯 Daily Practice Routine

### Morning (10 minutes)
1. Read through all 8 objection responses
2. Practice saying them OUT LOUD (not just reading)
3. Pick one scenario and role-play it

### Before Calling (5 minutes)
1. Review the objection you expect most
2. Have your response ready
3. Smile before dialing (seriously - it changes your tone!)

### After Each Call (2 minutes)
1. Write down what objection came up
2. Note how you handled it
3. What would you do differently next time?

### End of Day (10 minutes)
1. Review all calls
2. Identify patterns (what objections keep coming up?)
3. Practice those responses for tomorrow

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Pause after they speak** - Don't jump in immediately. Let them finish.

2. **Repeat their concern** - "So what I'm hearing is..." shows you're listening.

3. **Ask questions** - The person asking questions controls the conversation.

4. **Get the email** - Even if they say no, get their email for follow-up.

5. **Set specific follow-ups** - "I'll call Thursday" beats "I'll call sometime."

6. **Track everything** - You'll spot patterns and improve faster.

7. **Celebrate small wins** - First conversation, first follow-up, first sale!
